✨ | Music Commands
Name: Description:
/play 🎵 | Enter the song link or a song name to play music!
/stop 🛑 | Leave the current voice channel.
/loop 🔃 | Loop the current music.
/pause-resume-skip-back ⏸️ | Pause, resume, skip, or replay the past or current song.
/queue 📣 | Show the playlist.
/tts 💬 | Convert text to speech and play in a voice channel.
/remove 🚫 | Select a song to remove from the playlist.
/search 🔎 | Enter a song name to search.
/volume 🔊 | Configure the bot's volume.
/playlist 🎵 | Toggle your playlist.
/shuffle 🃏 | Shuffle the Playlist.
